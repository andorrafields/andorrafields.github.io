Project: Pet Play Pals

Description:

Pet Play Pals website allows users to view and join pet playdates nearby.
They can also create their own events and approve or reject attendees.
Each event has a forum for attendees to post questions and comments.
Users can register for an account and log in to add their pets and other info to their profile.
They can sign up individual pets or all of them to any chosen event.

Technology Used:

Vue, Spring MVC, RestFUL API, Google Maps API, Java, PostgreSQL
